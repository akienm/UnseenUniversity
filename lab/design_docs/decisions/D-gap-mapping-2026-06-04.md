## Decision narrative

The palace maps what we know. Nothing maps what we don't know. Akien's framing: the unmapped territory is where human intuition operates — when everything is mapped, there is no need for intuition. A system that can locate its own knowledge gaps would be closer to genuine understanding. The observable outcome of implementing gap mapping is unknown at filing time; we believe it reduces retries, but cannot yet measure that. This is intentional epistemic honesty. T-gap-mapping-research is a design exploration that must answer what 'known unknown' looks like mechanically before any implementation is attempted.

## Hypothesis
Unknown. We think fewer retries result from explicit gap awareness, but this is intuition, not a falsifiable claim. The research ticket must surface whether a falsifiable claim is even possible.
